Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI analysis? expand_more
Our AI models are trained on over 500,000 clinically validated images and achieve an 99.4% detection rate for initial biomarkers. However, this is a screening tool, not a final diagnostic result.
Will my insurance cover this analysis? expand_more
Many preventive health plans now cover AI-driven preliminary screenings. We provide a standardized medical receipt that you can submit for reimbursement to your provider.
What happens after I get my results? expand_more
The report will include clear categorized findings. If any areas of concern are detected, we provide a structured PDF summary specifically designed to be shared with your physician during a follow-up appointment.
Is my image data stored forever? expand_more
No. You have full control. By default, images are purged after 30 days unless you choose to store them in your secure health vault for long-term tracking.
